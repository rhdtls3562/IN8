About SOUND WAVE

SOUND WAVE is an independent music publishing company established in 2020, opening new horizons in the music industry with innovative artists.

We prioritize the creative freedom of our artists and support the full realization of their vision. Our goal is to create music that can touch listeners around the world.

Start your musical journey with SOUND WAVE, where passion for music and artistic value are our priorities.
